Event Info

Wednesday Wannamaker Worlds is a weekly short-track mountain bike race series held every Wednesday in August at Wannamaker North Trail in Goose Creek, SC. The course is fun and fast, designed for all skill levels, with race categories for beginners, intermediates, advanced riders, and kids on 24" wheels or smaller. Class 1 e-bikes are welcome and will race with the A race group. These bikes are pedal-assist only, limited to 20mph, and will be scored separately.

Race Format

  • Short‑track racing format with fast laps on cross-country terrain
  • Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced race classes
  • Each race lasts 20–25 minutes + 1 final lap
  • Kid-friendly course for young riders (24" wheels and under)
  • No race license required
  • Races are not eligible for upgrade points or national ranking
  • Keep your number plate for the full series — $5 replacement fee if lost

Weekly Race Schedule (Every Wednesday in August)

  • 4:00 PM – Course opens for practice
  • 5:00 PM – C Race (Beginner) – $25 – Trophies
  • 5:45 PM – Kids Race (24" wheels & under, age <13 free) – Trophies
  • 6:15 PM – B Race (Intermediate) – $25 – Trophies
  • 7:00 PM – A Race (Advanced + Class 1 e-bikes) – $25 – Trophies

Note: Kids under 13 (as of August 2025) race free. All others: $25 per race.
