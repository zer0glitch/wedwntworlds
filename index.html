Event Info

Wednesday Wannamaker Worlds is a weekly short-track mountain bike racing series held every Wednesday in August at Wannamaker North Trail in Goose Creek, SC. The course is fun and fast, with categories for all skill levels—from kids to beginners, intermediates, and advanced riders.

Race Format

  • Short‑track racing format with fast laps on cross-country terrain
  • Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced race classes
  • Each race lasts 20–25 minutes + 1 final lap
  • Kid-friendly course for young riders (24" wheels and under)

Weekly Race Schedule (Every Wednesday in August)

  • 4:00 PM – Course opens for practice
  • 5:00 PM – C Race (Beginner) – $25 – Trophies
  • 5:45 PM – Kids Race (24" wheels & under, age <13 free) – Trophies
  • 6:15 PM – B Race (Intermediate) – $25 – Trophies
  • 7:00 PM – A Race (Advanced) – $25 – Trophies

Note: Kids under 13 (as of August 2025) race free. All others: $25 per race.

Location

Wannamaker North Trail, Goose Creek, SC

Use the Westview Blvd entrance for parking and access to the trailhead.

Registration

Pre-register online for $25 per race. Kids under 13 race free. Trophies for each category winner.
